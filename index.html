Who We Are

A Unified Wellness Ecosystem

Penta Holding is a diversified health and wellness group that integrates every element of the value chain — from product development and manufacturing partnerships to digital marketing, logistics, and consumer engagement.

Founded in 2025, we unite multiple specialized companies under a single vision: delivering smarter, high-quality wellness solutions tailored for the MENA region. We operate with agility and innovation, ensuring that each step — from idea to impact — meets the evolving demands of today's health-conscious consumers.
